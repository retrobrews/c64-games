Markanoid is a Commodore 64 game developed by Ice Team released in 2003 for the 4KB Minigame Competition where it reached the 6° position.

The game has 14 levels, powerups and music even if it has a size of only 4KB .

Source code is inside the download file.

Use joystick in port 1 and possible a 8580 sid chip.
